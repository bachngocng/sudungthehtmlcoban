Character Spotlight: BOROS

Nhap vao day

Boros (ボロス, Borosu), also called Lord Boros (ボロス様, Borosu-sama) by his subordinates,
was the leader of the Dark Matter Thieves, a group of alien invaders responsible for the destruction of A-City.
Like Saitama, Boros faced a self-imposed existential crisis, having become so powerful that no battle thrilled him.
